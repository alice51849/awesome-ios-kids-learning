# Awesome iOS Kids Learning

## Phonics & Reading

- [Khan Academy Kids](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1378467217) - Broad early-learning app with stories, phonics, math, and social-emotional activities.
- [Duolingo ABC](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1440502656) - Free literacy app for early readers with letter sounds and short reading lessons.
- [Endless Alphabet](https://apps.apple.com/app/id591626572) - Playful vocabulary and letter-sound practice with animated words.
- [Starfall ABCs](https://apps.apple.com/app/id395623983) - Classic phonics app for letter recognition and beginning sounds.
- [Lumi Letters Lite](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6778748533) - Lumi Studio entry-level literacy app for practicing early letter skills. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumiletters.html)
- [Lumi Letters Pro](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6778491147) - Lumi Studio expanded literacy app for structured letter-learning practice. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumiletterspro.html)

## Math

- [Todo Math](https://apps.apple.com/app/id666465255) - Accessible early math practice covering counting, operations, and problem solving.
- [DragonBox Numbers](https://apps.apple.com/app/id920464806) - Visual number-sense game for young learners.
- [Prodigy Math Game](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1206398008) - Curriculum-aligned math practice wrapped in a game world.
- [Moose Math](https://apps.apple.com/app/id513533105) - Duck Duck Moose math games for counting, addition, subtraction, and shapes.
- [Lumi Math Planet](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6778269699) - Lumi Studio math app with a planet-themed practice flow for kids. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumimath.html)
- [Lumi Math Planet Pro](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6776958488) - Lumi Studio pro version for additional math practice and progression. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumimathpro.html)

## Chinese & Bopomofo

- [Chineasy Cards](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1279461908) - Visual flashcards that introduce Chinese characters through illustrations.
- [Skritter Chinese](https://apps.apple.com/app/id600354942) - Writing-focused Chinese practice with stroke feedback and spaced repetition.
- [LingoDeer](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1261193709) - Structured Asian-language lessons, including Chinese, with grammar and vocabulary practice.
- [Pleco Chinese Dictionary](https://apps.apple.com/app/id341922306) - Reference app useful for families learning Chinese vocabulary and pronunciation.
- [Lumi Bopomofo](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6773017109) - Lumi Studio app for practicing Zhuyin/Bopomofo basics. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumibopomofo.html)
- [Lumi Bopomofo Pro](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6775773117) - Lumi Studio pro version for extended Bopomofo learning practice. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumibopomofopro.html)

## Routines & Habits

- [Brili Routines](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1050204218) - Visual routine manager designed to help kids follow daily tasks.
- [Happy Kids Timer](https://apps.apple.com/app/id928895246) - Timer-based morning and bedtime routine app for children.
- [Joon](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1482225727) - Habit and task app that turns routines into a kid-friendly game.
- [Lumi Mission Planet](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6779750237) - Lumi Studio app for kid-friendly missions, routines, and habit building. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumimission.html)
- [Lumi Mission Planet Pro](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6779745474) - Lumi Studio pro version for expanded routine and mission features. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumimissionpro.html)

## Weather & Science

- [NASA](https://apps.apple.com/app/id334325516) - Official NASA app with images, missions, videos, and space news.
- [The Human Body by Tinybop](https://apps.apple.com/app/id682046579) - Interactive introduction to body systems for curious kids.
- [MarcoPolo Weather](https://apps.apple.com/app/id916365675) - Kid-friendly weather exploration through interactive scenes.
- [SkyView Lite](https://apps.apple.com/app/id413936865) - Simple augmented-reality sky guide for identifying stars and planets.
- [Lumi Weather](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6779552704) - Lumi Studio child-friendly weather app for building everyday weather awareness. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/lumiweather.html)
